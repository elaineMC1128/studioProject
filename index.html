Instructions

  • Press start to begin the pool movement and sound.
  • Drag sea creatures from the sand into a pitch ring.
  • Inner, middle, and outer rings play high, mid, and low pitches.
  • Use the sliders to change the high and mid pitch areas.
  • Creatures make sounds when they collide with walls or each other.
  • Click a creature in the pool to remove it. Press clean to clear all.
